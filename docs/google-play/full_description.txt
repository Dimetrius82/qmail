Q-Mail is an open source email client with support for multiple accounts, search, Exchange, IMAP push email, multi-folder sync,
flagging, filing, signatures, BCC-self, PGP/MIME, S/MIME & more!

Q-Mail supports IMAP, POP3 , Exchange 2003/2007 with WebDAV and later versions with EWS.

Install the app "OpenKeychain: Easy PGP" to encrypt/decrypt your emails using OpenPGP.

Q-Mail is a community developed project.  If you're interested in helping to making the
email client on Android even better, please join us!  You can find our bug tracker, source code, mailing list
and wiki at https://github.com/philipwhiuk/q-mail
We're always happy to welcome new developers, designers, documenters, bug triagers and friends. 

If you're having trouble with Q-Mail, please report a bug at https://github.com/philipwhiuk/q-mail rather than just
leaving a one-star review. We don't mind you telling the world that you're frustrated, but if you use our bug tracker,
we have a better chance of fixing whatever's giving you a hard time.

You can find Q-Mail's release notes at: http://philipwhiuk.github.io/qmail/releaseNotes.html
